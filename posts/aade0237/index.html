介绍

【考察要求】

在40分钟内用至少250个词对所给的论题进行立场阐明、论点展开和论据支持，要求快速审题定位，构思思路，论证观点

【作文要求】

【整体框架】

  1. 引言：改写题干，清晰地表明立场或要讨论的范围
  2. 主体段1：支持论点的理由和例证
  3. 主体段2：支持论点的理由和例证
  4. 结论：重申立场，总结观点，提出呼吁

【论题类型】

  • 同意与否：给出一个观点，是同意、不同意还是部分同意
  • 利弊评价：分析某个现象的利与弊，并给出总体态度
  • 双边讨论：给出的两个不一致的观点，描述自己的看法
  • 解决方案：指出某社会／环境问题的原因，并提出解决方法

【主题类型】

  • 教育：传统学习、应试教育、早期教育、同伴压力、终身学习
  • 环境：气候变化、塑料污染、垃圾分类、生物多样性、水资源管理、城市绿化
  • 健康：肥胖问题、公共医疗、心理健康、运动健身、饮食文化
  • 科技：人工智能、社交媒体、自动化、隐私保护、在线购物
  • 社会：人口老龄化、城乡差距、贫富差异、社会保障、性别平等
  • 经济：创业支持、兼职工作、本地就业、通货膨胀
  • 文化：文化保护、名牌文化、媒体报道、语言多样性
  • 犯罪：青少年犯罪、严格执法、惩罚机制、网络犯罪
  • 交通：过度旅游、公共交通、共享出行、交通堵塞
  • 社区：单亲家庭、代际冲突、志愿活动、数字社区
  • 全球：国际贸易、卫生合作、移民政策、能源安全

句型

正面影响和反面影响

【引入】

With [正在发生的现象], it has sparked a lasting debate over whether [双边讨论的主题]. While I acknowledge its considerable benefits such as [正面影响], I also have to contend that it poses risks to [反面影响].

【总结】

In conclusion, although [讨论对象] offer undeniable advantages in terms of [正面影响], they also threaten to [反面影响]. Ultimately, a well-designed and balanced approach will best serve people’s needs.

部分认可最好方法

【引入】

It is often argued that [题目方法及其目标]. I partially agree with this view, given that [原因], while other approaches such as [其他方法] are also crucial for [功效].

【总结】

All things considered, although [题目方法] is a considerable step toward [目标], it must be complemented by [其他方法]. Only by combining such strategies can we achieve [目标].

新事物和旧事物

【引入】

With [现象], it is argued that conventional [旧事物], which were once [旧情况], have outlived its usefulness. As for me, I partially agree with this view: although [旧事物] struggles to [缺点], it still plays a vital role in [优点].

【总结】

In conclusion, even though [旧事物] have undeniable shortcomings, certain benefits such as [优点] cannot be replicated by purely [新事物]. Therefore, it is necessary to retain [旧事物], provided we can fully utilize their distinct strengths.

问题的原因和对策

【引入】

Even though [现象], a great number of people [现象]. This trend persists primarily because [原因]. Nonetheless, there are still some effective solutions, such as [做法], to reverse the situation.

【总结】

In conclusion, many factors contribute to [现象], and eliminating [现象] requires more than individual willpower. Therefore, pairing [做法] with [做法] can [效果] and subsequently improve [效果] on a broad scale.

范文

双边讨论

With many scientists considering that computers will eventually outperform human beings, it has sparked a lasting debate over whether this development has a positive impact or is harmful to humanity. While I acknowledge its considerable benefits such as promoting efficiency and alleviating workloads, I also contend that it poses serious risks to human creativity and employment.

On the one hand, intelligent computers can substantially cut the time required for routine work. With the aid of various algorithms, humans can process vast volumes of data quickly and accurately, which took much longer by hand before, resulting in abundant free time for leisure and exercise. For instance, a recent study by a leading research team has found that students using modern computers complete the same text task twice as fast as those using machines from ten years ago, with significantly higher correctness and stability.

On the other hand, overreliance on advanced computers may undermine our own cognitive abilities and creativity. As individuals defer more decisions to technical models, they risk losing intellectual skills, internal motivation and original thoughts. Moreover, given that computers are capable of taking over complex assignments in the future, the number of positions will slump, while many jobs may even be eliminated and totally replaced by automation, leading to widespread unemployment and social panic. Consequently, people are about to suffer devastating economic setbacks and technology companies may be regarded as irresponsible and selfish for falling to mitigate family burdens.

In conclusion, although smart computers offer undeniable advantages in terms of productivity and pressure reduction, they also threaten to erode human creative capacity and job security. Therefore, only by embracing their capacity to address challenges and investing in lifelong learning can we ensure that we remain valued.

同意与否

It is often argued that narrowing the gap between the richest and the poorest can help create a harmonious society. I partially agree with this view, given that smaller differences do substantially promote social cohesion and alleviate individual anxiety, while other approaches such as expanding employment opportunities, increasing basic incomes and reducing regional disparities are also crucial for boosting overall satisfaction.

To begin with, it is proved that people tend to feel more secure and hopeful living in a community where their financial situations hover around the median income level. By contrast, those who regard themselves at the bottom of the income scale often experience a sense of frustration. Moreover, if individual economic differences are reduced or even eliminated, people are more likely to hold positive attitudes towards future setbacks, resulting in social solidarity and stability. These positive effects underscore why income compression matters.

Nevertheless, more essential measures must be underlined for addressing the root causes of social inequality. For example, evidence from the data released by the United Nations shows that the proportion of people who are satisfied with their own lives soared from 17% to approximately 80% in East Asia between 1999 and 2019, thanks to expanded employment programs and higher baseline incomes made by governments. Beyond these economic interventions, mitigating regional disparities also plays a key role, for this guarantees that both core cities and remote areas enjoy equal prospects for advancement, thereby making people feel valued.

All things considered, although reducing wealth inequality is a considerable step toward social harmony, it must be complemented by measures that empower the poor to improve their incomes and ensure equitable resource allocation. Only by combining such strategies together can we build a happier society in which everyone thrives.

利弊评价

With advanced technology becoming increasingly available for education, it is argued that conventional lectures, which were once the standard method of teaching large classes, have outlived their usefulness. As for me, I partially agree with this view: although lectures struggle to attract students and offer no means of review, they still play a vital role in real-time interaction and are more cost-effective.

On the one hand, lectures are often criticized because if students miss a single point, they may be confused about the following content, with no way to revisit it. By contrast, videos on modern online platforms allow students to replay lectures at any time and in any place. Moreover, student concentration cannot be neglected, since they tend to get distracted by peers sitting next to them. One of the most significant disadvantages of lectures is that their format is so limited that there is hardly any innovation or creativity, leading to boredom and disappointment among students. These drawbacks indeed explain why many hold negative attitudes towards lectures.

On the other hand, lectures still have irreplaceable benefits that should be underscored. For instance, they provide an excellent opportunity for students to interact with teachers, as questions can be answered instantly on the spot. In addition, online lessons require access to expensive computers or smartphones, whereas lectures simply require physical presence. By comparison, lectures are far cheaper than modern technology, thereby promoting equality and fairness. Furthermore, when we consider efficiency, it is notable that students often learn more in offline lectures than in online sessions. A recent experiment by a leading research group found that students who attended a lecture in a school hall scored significantly higher on a subsequent test than those who viewed the same lecture online at home.

In conclusion, while lectures have undeniable shortcomings, certain benefits such as immediate interaction and cost savings cannot be replicated by purely advanced technology. Therefore, it is necessary to retain lectures, provided we can fully utilize their distinct strengths.

解决措施

Even though scientific research has proved that certain habits such as smoking, excessive drinking and overeating are harmful to human health, a great number of people keep engaging in those unhealthy activities. This trend persists primarily because they offer immediate gratification, provide an easy way to unwind and have become prevalent. Nonetheless, there are still some effective solutions, such as more standardized regulations and advertising slogans, to reverse the situation.

Turning first to the causes, many people prioritize the immediate pleasure over long-term well-being. For example, a sugary snack or a cigarette break deliver great amusement in a second, compared to regular exercise and healthy diets, whose effects are indirect, subtle and abstract. Moreover, social influences reinforce these harmful choices, since people suffering from peer pressure or family burdens barely have little way to relax themselves apart from drinking alcohol and overeating. Last but not least, such unsound activities are so common in social venues that people cannot help but participate in them or are easily persuaded to try some by others, although they may be reluctant at first.

To address these root causes, companies have to launch massive campaigns, incorporating graphic warnings and visible slogans to raise awareness. Furthermore, governments play a vital role in tackling the problems by implementing regulatory interventions such as higher taxes and stricter laws. With efficient implementation, people’s attitudes towards lives will shift accordingly. As reported by the National Bureau of Statistics, the youth smoking rate fell from 20% to 7% within ten months in 2018 when the tobacco agency introduced more warning labels on the package and raised taxes by 10%.

In conclusion, many factors contribute to unhealthy lifestyles, and eliminating harmful behaviors requires more than individual willpower. Therefore, pairing impactful warning labels with robust regulations can encourage people to adopt more beneficial habits and subsequently improve public health on a broad scale.

万能模板

  1. 开头描述背景信息

  • In recent years, there has been a growing trend of …

  • It is becoming increasingly common for people to …

  • With the rapid development of …

  1. 改写题目所给的论点

  • It has sparked a debate over whether …

  • While others believe that …, some argue that …

  1. 输出自己的观点

  • From my perspective, I partially agree with this view for …

  • In my point of view, although I acknowledge that …, I still contend that …

  • On balance, I am inclined to believe that …

  1. 总结对题目论点的评价

  • In conclusion, there are convincing and justified arguments on both aspects.

  • To sum up, each viewpoint has its own merits, and neither can be completely dismissed.

  1. 再次表达自己的观点

  • Although … is a considerable step toward …, it must be complemented by …

  • Although … have undeniable shortcomings in terms of …, certain benefits such as … cannot be replicated by purely …

  • Although … has undeniable advantages with regard to …, it also threatens to …

  1. 提出呼吁并说明意义

  • Therefore, as far as I am concerned, a balanced approach that combines … and … would be most effective and consequently benefit … on a broad scale…

  • Therefore, only by … can we eliminate the root cause of … and achieve …
